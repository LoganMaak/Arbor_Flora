Arbor & Flora

Arbor and Flora is a team of designers and naturalists dedicated to bringing wild elements into peoples lives with a thoughtful and sustainable approach. Foraging from our local flora, farms, and friends we are striving to show how beautiful the world is right outside your front door. We are currently working on bringing nature in through large scale designs, seasonal wild wreath making, and supporting our local artists and economy.

Meet the Makers

Hannah is the designer, after studying horticulture and graduating from Longwood Gardens, she began focusing primarily on floral design. She enjoys large scale installations that encompass the viewers eyes and environment.

Matthew is the naturalist, learning about the local ecology through exploration. He is set on making this world more beautiful by developing and protecting our landscapes.
